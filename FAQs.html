FAQs

1. Should I come prepared with a business idea of my own?

No, you’ll have to come up with a business idea according to the problem statements we’ll be providing you in the competition.

2. Should I have complete knowledge of stock market for participating in this event?

Not necessarily. We will be helping you to understand stock market to you from scratch so that it comes handy to you in the competition and in future.

3. Is it necessary to register in teams?

No, registrations will be strictly through the gravitas registration portal and only individual registration in allowed there. But if you want to participate in a team, all the team members can register individually and then inform us before the competition about your team and its member.

4. What can be the maximum team strength?

A team can have maximum of 5 members.
